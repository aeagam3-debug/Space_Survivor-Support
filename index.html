Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

Star Survivor saves automatically as you play. Your Stardust and permanent upgrades are stored on your device.

I lost my progress after deleting the app.

Saves are stored locally, so uninstalling the app removes them. We don't currently support cloud saves.

How do I change the language?

Tap Options on the main menu and pick your language from the dropdown. English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, and Turkish are supported.

The game runs slowly on my device.

Star Survivor is optimized for recent devices. If you're seeing slowdown during large hordes, please email us with your device model so we can investigate.

Are my Stardust purchases real money?

No — Stardust is earned in-game and spent on permanent upgrades. There are no in-app purchases.
